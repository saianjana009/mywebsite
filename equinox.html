Equinox Group is an American luxury fitness company which operates several separate lifestyle brands: Equinox, Equinox Hotels, Precision Run, Project by Equinox, Equinox Explore, Equinox Media, Furthermore, PURE Yoga, Blink Fitness, and SoulCycle. Within Equinox's portfolio of brands, there are more than 300 locations within major cities in the United States, as well as in London, Toronto, and Vancouver.
